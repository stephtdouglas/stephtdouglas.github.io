About Me

I'm an observational astronomer focusing on the evolution of stellar rotation. I approach this problem from three different angles: 1) calibrating angular momentum evolution models by measuring rotation periods in open clusters, 2) investigating the connection between rotation and magnetic activity, and 3) comparing the rotational evolution of single stars and binaries.

I'm currently an NSF Astronomy & Astrophysics Postdoctoral Fellow working at the Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian.
